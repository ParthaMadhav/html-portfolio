Egg Maggie

Ingredients:

    ooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooooo
  • 1.2 Maggie Packs
  • 1 Tb spoon of Chilli powder
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt(as per consumer choice)
  • Coriander
  • Pepper powder
  • Chopped chillies

Procedure

Steps

  1. Take a Bowl and add 500 ml of water and then boil it
  2. After boiling ,Cut open the maggie packets and put the noodles in the boiling water
  3. now take ann bowl and add 2 tbspn of oil and put the chopped tomatoes and onions
  4. let the onions and tomatoes boil add chopped chillies
  5. crack open the 2 eggs into the bowl
  6. add chilli powder (optional) and pepper powder and salt to it
  7. Add maggi masala to the mixture and ix it well
  8. Now,Add the boiled noodles into the mixture
  9. lastly,add chopped coriander and serve it into a plate

Now, our Egg maggie is Ready to eat
